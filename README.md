# Sales & Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance and customer purchasing behaviors across product categories, geographic regions, and payment methods. It helps business teams uncover revenue trends, understand customer preferences, and improve operational efficiency.

# Dashboard Objectives

The dashboard aims to help retail stakeholders:
- Track sales growth and seasonal demand patterns over time
- Identify top-performing product categories driving revenue
- Understand customer distribution across regions and assess market reach
- Analyse payment preferences to improve checkout experience
- Investigate delivery performance issues to improve operational reliability

These insights support data-driven decisions in marketing, fulfillment, and product strategy.

# Business Scenario

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transaction data. To stay competitive, they must quickly identify:
- What customers are buying
- When peak purchasing periods occur
- Where sales are concentrated
- How customers complete their payments
- Which fulfillment bottlenecks impact delivery performance

This dashboard turns raw sales data into clear insights so business teams can better forecast demand, optimize inventory, and streamline customer experience.

# Target Users & Needs
- Sales Managers: Category performance, monthly revenue trends, revenue drivers
- Operations Managers: Delivery status monitoring, delay risks
- Customer: Insights Analysts	Customer distribution and purchasing behaviors

All users require real-time access to revenue metrics, regional breakdowns, and delivery efficiency indicators.
